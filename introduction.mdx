## What is AMH Sistema de Oficios?

AMH Sistema de Oficios is a web-based document management platform built for municipal government operations. It digitizes the full lifecycle of official documents — from creation and routing to assignment, response, and archival — replacing paper-based workflows with a traceable, role-driven system.

The platform connects four user roles in a structured chain of custody:

- **Gestores** create and send oficios to the relevant department
- **Subdirectores** receive, review, and assign oficios to their JUDs
- **JUDs** (Jefes de Unidad Departamental) process and respond to assigned oficios
- **Administrators** oversee users, roles, and the complete document archive

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Role-based dashboards" icon="gauge-high">
    Each role sees a personalized dashboard with relevant KPIs, pending work, and action queues
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic PDF stamping" icon="stamp">
    Uploaded PDFs receive an automatic receipt stamp (acuse de recibido) with folio, date, and recipient
  </Card>
  <Card title="Email notifications" icon="envelope">
    Automatic emails at every workflow step keep all parties informed without manual follow-up
  </Card>
  <Card title="Peticiones workflow" icon="paper-plane">
    JUDs and Subdirectores can submit formal requests up the hierarchy with full document support
  </Card>
  <Card title="Consecutive folio numbering" icon="list-ol">
    Every oficio receives a unique sequential folio number per year, generated with concurrency safety
  </Card>
  <Card title="File attachment support" icon="paperclip">
    Attach PDF, DOC, Excel, and image files to oficios and peticiones with secure server-side storage
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
